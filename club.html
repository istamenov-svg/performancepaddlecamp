The Program

We Bring the System to You

Performance Paddle Camp travels to clubs across the country. We bring our coaching team, our curriculum, and our system to your courts. Your members receive the same world-class instruction that's made our Nantucket camp the longest-running paddle camp in America.

Our approach is collaborative. We work with your existing staff, share our drill formats and programming, and create continuity of instruction that lasts well beyond the camp visit. Your members get a wealth of knowledge; your pros get expanded tools for the season ahead.

What We Bring

✓  Customized curriculum based on your members' level and goals

✓  On-court coaching from nationally ranked professionals

✓  Drill library and season programming your pro staff can continue using

✓  Clinic formats for beginner through advanced players

✓  Flexible scheduling to fit your club calendar

The Process

How It Works

1. You Build Your Group

Gather your players — typically 4 to 16. Whether it's a league team, a group of competitive friends, or your entire club membership, you decide who's in.

2. You Coordinate Courts

Reserve court time at your club or facility. We'll work with whatever setup you have — from a single court to a full multi-court block.

3. We Bring the System

Our coaching staff arrives with the full Performance Paddle methodology — drills, strategy sessions, and personalized instruction tailored to your group's level and goals.

Who It's For

Ideal For

League Teams

Get your squad tournament-ready before the season starts. We focus on doubles strategy, shot selection under pressure, and building team chemistry on the court.

Club Season Kick-Off Camp

Launch your club's season with a high-energy camp that sharpens skills, builds excitement, and sets the competitive tone for the months ahead.

Competitive Friend Groups

You already play together every week. Now take it up a level with structured coaching that targets the specific gaps holding your group back.

Tournament-Focused Players

Serious players preparing for APTA events or regional tournaments. We drill match scenarios, mental toughness, and the high-percentage patterns that win tight sets.

Women's or Men's Paddle Weekends

Turn a social weekend into a weekend that actually improves your game. Intensive instruction during the day, camaraderie off the court.

Clubs Seeking High-Level Instruction

Want elite coaching without the logistics of hosting a full camp? We bring the expertise and curriculum — you just open the courts.
